# Google Play — Data Safety Section

**Privacy Policy URL:** https://noetia.app/legal/privacy

> Copy the answers below into the Data Safety form in Google Play Console.
> Section: Store presence → Data safety

---

## Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?

**Yes**

---

## Is all of the user data collected by your app encrypted in transit?

**Yes** — All data is transmitted over TLS 1.2 or higher.

---

## Do you provide a way for users to request that their data is deleted?

**Yes** — Users can request data deletion by emailing legal@noetia.app with the subject "Delete my account". We will process the request within 30 days.

## Data Types Collected

### Personal info

| Data type | Collected | Shared | Required or optional | Purpose(s) |
|-----------|-----------|--------|----------------------|------------|
| Name | Yes | No | Required | Account functionality |
| Email address | Yes | No | Required | Account functionality, app functionality |

### Financial info

| Data type | Collected | Shared | Required or optional | Purpose(s) |
|-----------|-----------|--------|----------------------|------------|
| Purchase history | Yes | Yes — shared with Stripe (payment processor) | Required | Analytics, Account management |

*Note: Noetia does not handle or store raw payment card data. Payment processing is delegated entirely to Stripe.*

### App activity

| Data type | Collected | Shared | Required or optional | Purpose(s) |
|-----------|-----------|--------|----------------------|------------|
| App interactions | Yes | No | Required | App functionality, Analytics |
| In-app search history | No | — | — | — |
| Installed apps | No | — | — | — |
| Other user-generated content | Yes (reading fragments, notes) | No | Optional | App functionality |

### App info and performance

| Data type | Collected | Shared | Required or optional | Purpose(s) |
|-----------|-----------|--------|----------------------|------------|
| Crash logs | Yes | No | Required | Analytics (app improvement) |
| Diagnostics | No | — | — | — |

## Data sharing with third parties

| Third party | Data shared | Purpose |
|-------------|-------------|---------|
| Stripe | Purchase history, billing email | Payment processing |
| Google (OAuth) | Email, name | Sign-in authentication |
| Facebook (OAuth) | Email, name | Sign-in authentication |
| Apple (OAuth) | Email | Sign-in authentication |

**We do not sell user data to any third party.**

## Data retention and deletion

| Data category | Retention period |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Account data (name, email) | Until user requests account deletion |
| Reading progress and fragments | Until user requests account deletion |
| Payment records | 7 years (required by tax law) |
| Crash logs | 90 days |
