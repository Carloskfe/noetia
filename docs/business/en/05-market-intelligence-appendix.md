# Noetia — Market Intelligence Appendix
**Version 1.0 | May 2026**

---

## Purpose

This appendix provides full citations, methodology notes, and confidence ratings for every quantitative market claim in the Noetia Business Plan. It exists so that every number can be traced to a primary source before investor presentations, fundraising decks, or press materials.

**Confidence ratings used:**
- ✅ **Verified** — Direct citation from a named report, with year and scope confirmed
- ⚠️ **Estimated** — Industry consensus or composite calculation; methodology disclosed
- ❌ **Needs verification** — Claim could not be confirmed against a primary source; flagged for removal or replacement

| **Value cited** | $35.47 billion by 2030 |

| **Scope** | Global; all audiobook formats (streaming + downloadable); all distribution channels |

**How to cite in documents:** "The global audiobook market was valued at $8.70 billion in 2024 and is projected to reach $35.47 billion by 2030, growing at a CAGR of 26.2% (Grand View Research, 2024)."

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Value cited** | ~130 million |
| **Derivation** | 413M mobile internet users × ~32% digital content engagement rate |

**Recommended upgrade:** Commission or license a Statista custom report on "digital reading engagement in Latin America" for a single-source citation.

### Claim: Paid digital reading segment: 8–12M users in Latin America, growing ~18% annually

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Value cited** | 8–12 million paying users; ~18% annual growth |
| **Derivation** | Latin America e-commerce users: 290M in 2024 (Statista/eMarketer) × estimated 3–4% digital book buyer penetration rate = 8.7–11.6M → rounded to 8–12M |
| **Growth rate source** | Mordor Intelligence, "E-Book Market" report (2024): regional digital content subscription CAGR for Latin America |
| **Confidence** | ⚠️ Estimated — built from e-commerce base rates. No single report directly sizes the "paying Spanish-language digital reader" segment. This range is directionally correct but should be treated as an order-of-magnitude estimate. |

**Recommended upgrade:** Cross-reference with Audio Publishers Association Latin America data or commission primary research before Series A materials.
